## Expanding This Adventure

To turn this into a multi-session adventure, you could make the hog heist part of a series of efforts to destabilize Tashgarn’s oversight. Characters could conduct sabotage, spread rumors, or cause friction between the Fused and his conscripted soldiers. These actions could lead to an attack by the Herdazian resistance!

n ancient temple to the Passions in Longbrow’s Straits off the coast of Thaylenah houses a perfect gemstone, locked away behind a maze. The temple’s guardian might part with the gemstone if the party can maneuver the temple’s maze and prove their passions. This intellectual puzzle features a conversation and an endeavor.
