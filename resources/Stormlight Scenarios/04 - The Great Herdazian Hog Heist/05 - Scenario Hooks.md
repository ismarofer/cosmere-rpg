## Scenario Hooks

Use one of the following adventure hooks to introduce your players to the scenario, or develop a hook of your own:

Under Employ. Taravangian sent the PCs to

recruit the temple’s guardian and acquire the perfect gemstone. I Heard a Rumor. The PCs heard about the perfect

gemstone and are posing as Coalition representatives to take it for themselves.
