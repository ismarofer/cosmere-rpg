## Conclusion and Rewards

Depending on the party’s decisions, successes, and failures, any of the following might apply:

Passions Proved. If the PCs succeed in Prawf’s

final trial, they receive the perfect gemstone. If the “Gaining Entry” conversation also succeeded, Prawf offers the party her alliance out of gratitude for confirming her beliefs. Faithless. If the PCs failed to pass Prawf’s final test,

she slips away with the perfect gemstone through a secret passageway.
