## Expanding This Adventure

To turn this into a multi-session adventure, you might follow the party as they wrestle with the fallout from the final trial. If the party succeeded, they may be hunted for the gemstone; possible pursuers could include Veden soldiers, Odium’s forces, or enemies of Prawf. Or if they failed, the PCs might pursue Prawf to steal the gemstone.
