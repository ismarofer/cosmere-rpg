## Conclusion and Rewards

Depending on the party’s decisions, successes, and failures, any of the following might apply:

Mission Accomplished. If the PCs save Melur and

defeat Eltharon, Zoyah will lead the party to a supply cache containing a Shardblade. But at What Cost. If the PCs save only Melur or only

Zoyah, the surviving NPC promises the resistance will owe the party a favor. Total Loss. If Melur and Zoyah both die, the PCs

receive no reward.
