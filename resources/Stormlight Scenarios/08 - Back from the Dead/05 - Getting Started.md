## Getting Started

The Marabethian cliffs are treacherous escarpments rising high above the sea. Here, convicted criminals are hung by their feet to brave the elements and hungry yu-nerig—massive greatshells who wait for high tide to bring them easy prey. Odium-aligned clifftop patrols mean the only approach to the bluffs is by boat, and a looming highstorm threatens to dash the prisoner—and their boat—against the rocks. The PCs need to work quickly or lose their escape.
