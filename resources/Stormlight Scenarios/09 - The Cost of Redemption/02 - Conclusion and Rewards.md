## Conclusion and Rewards

Depending on the party’s decisions, successes, and failures, any of the following might apply:

A Daring Escape. If the PCs escape with the prisoner,

Talenai provides the location of a gemstone cache and intelligence regarding a convoy of Heavenly Ones transporting a full set of Shards to the Shattered Plains. Breaking the Law. If any Skybreakers retreated,

the party may have earned the ire of the Order of Skybreakers.
