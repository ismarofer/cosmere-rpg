## Expanding This Adventure

To expand this adventure, consider making the cliff ascent an endeavor featuring hungry yu-nerig, or making the party locate Talenai amongst a dozen swinging prisoners. Subsequent adventures could feature a strike against Odium’s supply lines, and Vosian’s report (or absence) could lead the Skybreakers to attempt to recapture Talenai.
